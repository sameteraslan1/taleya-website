Frequently asked

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Taleya → Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and request a refund for your most recent Taleya charge.

I restored my subscription but premium content is still locked.

Open the app → tap ⚙️ Settings → pass the parental gate → tap Restore Purchases. If the issue persists, email us with your Apple ID email and we'll help manually.

The app is in the wrong language.

Taleya has two separate languages: the app interface follows your iPhone's system language, and the story/song content is chosen in ⚙️ Settings → Content Language.

Audio isn't playing.

Check that your iPhone isn't muted (silent switch) and that volume is turned up. If the story audio starts then stops, another app may have taken over audio playback — reopen Taleya and tap play again.

My child's data — what do you collect?

Nothing personal. No names, no emails, no accounts. See our Privacy Policy for the full picture.

When you email us

To help us respond faster, please include:

  • Your iPhone/iPad model and iOS version
  • The Taleya app version (Settings → About)
  • A short description of what happened
  • A screenshot, if possible

If you email us from inside the app (⚙️ Settings → Contact Support), this info is filled in for you automatically.
